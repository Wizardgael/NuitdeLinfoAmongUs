If you're interested in working with us on merch, please read the Dual Wield Studios Fan Creation Policy first.

They also have further information for licensing to:

  • Independant or small creators
  • Businesses and manufacturers

Please note that even with a dedicated group, responses will be delayed. Please be patient with us as we gain our footing during these exciting times!
